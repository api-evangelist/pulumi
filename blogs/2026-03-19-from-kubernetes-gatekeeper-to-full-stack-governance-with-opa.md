Pulumi’s OPA (Open Policy Agent) support is now stable. The v1.1.0 release of pulumi-policy-opa makes OPA/Rego a first-class policy language for Pulumi with full feature parity alongside the native TypeScript and Python policy SDKs. Write Rego policies that validate any resource Pulumi manages, across AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes, and the rest of the provider ecosystem.
