Since the launch of Pulumi IAM with custom roles and scoped access tokens , organizations have been using fine-grained permissions to secure their automation and CI/CD pipelines. As teams scale to hundreds or thousands of stacks, environments, and accounts, the next challenge is applying those permissions efficiently. Today, we’re introducing three new capabilities to help you manage permissions more dynamically at scale: tag-based access control , team role assignments , and user role assignments .
