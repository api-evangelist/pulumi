Infrastructure work ranges from simple updates to complex multi-stack operations. For straightforward tasks, jumping straight to execution is often fine. But complex tasks benefit from deliberate upfront thinking: understanding what exists, identifying dependencies, and agreeing on an approach before anything changes.
