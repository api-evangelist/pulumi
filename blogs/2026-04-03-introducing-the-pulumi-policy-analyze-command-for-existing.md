You can now run policy packs against your existing stack state without running your Pulumi program or making provider calls. The new pulumi policy analyze command evaluates your current infrastructure against local policy packs directly, turning policy validation into a fast, repeatable check. Why this command matters Policy authoring and policy updates usually involve an iteration loop: Make a policy change.
