Pulumi Neo already understands your infrastructure: your code, your stacks, your state. Today we’re launching new capabilities that extend Neo’s reach in two directions: into the third-party systems your team uses to plan and observe, and out to the cloud CLIs that actually drive your infrastructure. The first half is MCP integrations: connections to Atlassian , Datadog , Honeycomb , Linear , PagerDuty , and Supabase that show up as tools Neo can call during a task .
